### 3.1 Cost Comparison Article Template

```
ARTICLE TEMPLATE: COST COMPARISON

hospitals, and recovery included.

TARGET KEYWORD: "[treatment] India", "[treatment] cost India"

STRUCTURE:

1. INTRODUCTION (150 words)
   - Hook: "Did you know [treatment] costs [X]x less in India?"

   - Why patients are choosing India

2. COST BREAKDOWN (300 words)

3. TOP HOSPITALS (300 words)

- Hospital websites
```

## 5. CONTENT PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### 5.1 Article Production Steps

```
STEP 1: KEYWORD RESEARCH (30 mins)
├── Identify target keyword
├── Check search volume (Ubersuggest/Ahrefs free)
├── Analyze top 5 competing articles
└── Note unique angles to cover

STEP 2: OUTLINE CREATION (20 mins)
├── Template selection (cost/ country/ story)
├── Section by section outline
├── Unique points to cover
└── Internal link opportunities

STEP 3: WRITING (60-90 mins)
├── First draft without editing
├── Focus on value, not perfection
├── Include data points and examples
└── Write compelling intro + conclusion

STEP 4: SEO OPTIMIZATION (20 mins)
├── Add target keyword in:
│   ├── Title (beginning)
│   ├── First 100 words
│   ├── H2 headings
│   └── Meta description
├── Add supporting keywords naturally
├── Internal links to existing content
└── External links (hospitals, gov sites)

STEP 5: FORMATTING (15 mins)
├── Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
├── Bullet points for lists
├── Bold key phrases
├── Images with alt text
├── Table for cost comparisons
└── Clear CTAs

STEP 6: REVIEW & PUBLISH (10 mins)
├── Read aloud for flow
├── Check all links work
├── Mobile preview
├── Set featured image
└── Publish + social share
```

### 5.2 Content Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Title includes primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars) written
- [ ] Article is minimum 1,200 words
- [ ] At least 3 internal links
- [ ] At least 2 external links
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Cost table/cost section included
- [ ] Clear CTA at end
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] WhatsApp link included
