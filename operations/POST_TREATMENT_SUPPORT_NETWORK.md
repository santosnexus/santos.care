### 3.3 When to Recommend Homecare

```
RECOMMEND HOMEcare WHEN:
✓ Patient discharged but not fully independent
✓ Post-orthopedic surgery (knee, hip replacement)
✓ Post-cardiac surgery (need monitoring)
✓ Post-stroke (mobility limitations)
✓ Elderly patient traveling alone
✓ Patient with chronic conditions needing support
✓ Wound requiring daily dressing

COORDINATOR CHECKLIST:
□ Assess patient's care needs at discharge
□ Recommend appropriate service level
□ Coordinate with chosen agency
□ Brief caregiver on patient condition
□ Establish emergency contact
□ Daily check-in calls
□ Monitor care quality
```

## 8. COORDINATION CHECKLIST

### 8.1 Ayurveda Transfer Checklist

```
DISCHARGE TO AYUSH PRANA:

□ Patient medically cleared for transfer
□ Discharge summary obtained (English)
□ Medications for [X] days provided
□ Doctor's permission for travel
□ Ayurveda center pre-booked
□ Transport arranged
□ Coordinator accompanies (for first transfer)
□ Welcome message sent to Ayush Prana
□ Patient briefed on what to expect
□ Family notified of transfer
```

### 8.2 Homecare Setup Checklist

```
HOMECARE ARRANGEMENT:

□ Assess patient's care needs
□ Recommend appropriate service
□ Contact agency with patient details
□ Confirm booking and timing
□ Brief agency on patient condition
□ Arrange first visit at hospital or home
□ Provide emergency contact numbers
□ Confirm payment arrangement
□ Daily check-ins during service
□ Monitor quality and satisfaction
```

### 8.3 Lab Follow-Up Checklist

```
LAB COORDINATION:

□ Doctor orders required tests

□ Patient schedules test
□ Results shared with coordinator
□ Coordinator forwards to doctor
□ Telemedicine scheduled for interpretation
□ Any actions communicated to patient
```
