## 3. TRANSPORTATION PROTOCOLS

### 3.1 Airport Pickup Protocol

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
AIRPORT PICKUP STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

24 HOURS BEFORE ARRIVAL:
□ Confirm flight number and arrival time
□ Get PNR for flight tracking
□ Assign driver based on patient needs:
   - Standard: Regular patient
   - Premium: VIP or long-stay patients
   - Ambulance: Post-surgery or critical patients
□ Brief driver on:
   - Patient name
   - Special requirements
   - Hospital/hotel address
   - Flight number

4 HOURS BEFORE ARRIVAL:
□ Check flight status (delays?)
□ Confirm hotel booking
□ Prepare welcome kit in car:
   - Water bottles (2)
   - Wet wipes
   - Simple snack
   - Hospital documents folder
   - Welcome letter from Heal India
   - Mobile charger

AT ARRIVAL:
□ Driver at Arrivals Gate [X], [Specify gate]
□ Board displayed: "[PATIENT NAME] — HEAL INDIA MEDI TOURISM"
□ Driver WhatsApp to coordinator: "At arrivals, Gate X"
□ Coordinate with coordinator

UPON MEETING:
□ Warm welcome
□ Assist with luggage
□ Direct to vehicle
□ Offer water
□ Brief: "Welcome to Kerala! I'm [Driver Name]..."

IN TRANSIT:
□ Point out landmarks (builds comfort)
□ Ask about comfort level
□ Adjust AC temperature as needed
□ Brief schedule for next 24 hours

AT HOTEL:
□ Assist with luggage to entrance
□ Hand over to hotel staff
□ Brief hotel of dietary/medical needs
□ Confirm coordinator contact
□ Driver notifies coordinator: "Patient delivered"

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

### 3.2 Daily Hospital Transfer Protocol

```
DAILY TRANSFER SCHEDULE:

MORNING (Patient to Hospital):
□ Pickup from hotel: [Time] (coordinate with hotel breakfast)
□ Verify patient is ready
□ Assist into vehicle
□ Confirm: "Are you comfortable? Do you need anything?"
□ Drive to hospital
□ Park in designated patient area
□ Escort to International Patient Desk
□ Brief nurse of arrival
□ Confirm pickup time with patient

EVENING (Patient to Hotel):
□ Be at hospital by scheduled time
□ Notify coordinator of any delays
□ Meet patient at designated area
□ Assist into vehicle
□ Ask about day's experience
□ Drive to hotel
□ Confirm dinner plans
□ Evening coordinator check-in (WhatsApp)

COORDINATOR RESPONSIBILITIES:
□ Confirm morning pickup time previous evening
□ Be reachable by WhatsApp during transfers
□ Track all transfers in log
□ Escalate issues immediately
```

## 4. VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS & STANDARDS

```

✓ Air conditioning (functional, clean)

✓ Professional attire (formals or company uniform)
✓ Valid driving license
✓ GPS navigation knowledge
✓ Basic first aid training
✓ Punctual (arrives 15 mins early)
✓ Mobile: Always reachable
✓ Trained in patient handling

FOR CRITICAL PATIENTS:
✓ Stretcher capability (if applicable)
✓ Oxygen cylinder (for respiratory patients)
✓ Basic life support knowledge
✓ Communication with hospital
✓ Coordinated with ambulance if needed
```

## 7. COORDINATION CHECKLIST

### 7.1 Pre-Trip Transport Checklist

- [ ] Flight booked and confirmed
- [ ] Airport pickup arranged
- [ ] Hotel confirmed (address sent to patient)
- [ ] Driver briefed on patient details
- [ ] Welcome kit prepared in car
- [ ] WhatsApp group with patient + driver + coordinator

### 7.2 Daily Transport Checklist

- [ ] Morning pickup confirmed (previous evening)
- [ ] Patient ready when driver arrives
- [ ] Transfer logged (time, vehicle, route)
- [ ] Any issues noted and escalated
- [ ] Return transfer scheduled
