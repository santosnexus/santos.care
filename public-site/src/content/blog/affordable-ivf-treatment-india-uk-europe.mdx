## Why European Patients Are Choosing India for IVF

### 1. No Waiting Lists

### 2. Advanced Laboratory Technology
Indian fertility centres are equipped with:
- **ICSI** (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection) for male factor infertility
- **PGD/PGS** (Genetic Testing) for chromosomal abnormalities
- **Laser-Assisted Hatching** for improved implantation
- **Vitrification** — ultra-rapid freezing with 95%+ egg/survival rates

### 3. Higher Success Rates for Older Patients
For women over 38, Indian clinics report success rates of 35–45% per cycle with donor eggs — comparable to or better than UK average rates, at a fraction of the cost.

### 4. Surrogacy & Egg Donation Options
India has clear legal frameworks for both surrogacy and egg donation, with shorter waiting times than many European countries.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are Indian fertility clinics as regulated as European ones?**
Yes. India's Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) Act of 2021 regulates all fertility clinics. JCI-accredited centres follow international standards for laboratory protocols, infection control, and patient safety.

**Do I need a medical visa for IVF treatment in India?**
Yes. India offers a medical visa (M-visa) for fertility treatment. We provide all documentation including the hospital invitation letter. The visa is typically processed within 3–5 working days.

**Can I use an egg donor or surrogate in India?**
Yes. India allows anonymous and known egg donation. Surrogacy is legal for heterosexual married couples under the Surrogacy (Regulation) Act, with specific eligibility criteria.

**How many IVF cycles might I need?**
Success depends on age, diagnosis, and treatment protocol. Many clinics offer package pricing for multiple cycles. We recommend budgeting for 2–3 cycles maximum.

**What follow-up care is provided after I return to Europe?**
Your Indian fertility specialist will coordinate with your local gynaecologist and provide virtual follow-up consultations for the first trimester.

**Will my European health insurance cover IVF in India?**
Most European insurance does not cover IVF abroad, but the savings are so significant that even self-paying works out more affordable. Some international insurance plans do cover treatment — check with your provider.
