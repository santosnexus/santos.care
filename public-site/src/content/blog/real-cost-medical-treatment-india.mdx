## How to Avoid Hidden Costs

### 1. Get Everything in Writing

Ask your hospital or facilitator for a written, line-by-line quote that specifies:
- Exactly what's included (number of hospital days, ICU days, physio sessions)
- What's explicitly excluded
- The implant brand and model (if applicable)
- Payment schedule and refund policy

### 2. Ask About Complications Coverage

"What happens if I need an extra day in ICU?" Most packages cover standard recovery. Extended ICU stays or additional procedures may be billed separately. Clarify before you book.

### 3. Budget for Recovery, Not Just Surgery

The total time in India is typically 2–4 weeks. Your accommodation, meals, and incidental costs during recovery often equal the flight cost. Plan accordingly.

### 4. Carry a Contingency Fund

We recommend $500–$1,000 in accessible funds (credit card or cash) for unexpected needs — extended stay, additional tests, or last-minute medication refills.
