## Why African Patients Choose India for Heart Surgery

### World-Class Cardiac Surgeons
Indian cardiac surgeons are among the most experienced globally. Many have trained or practiced at leading US and UK institutions. A typical senior cardiac surgeon in Kochi has performed 5,000+ bypass procedures.

### JCI-Accredited Cardiac Centres
Our partner hospitals — Aster Medcity and Amrita Hospital in Kochi — are JCI-accredited, meeting the same international standards as the world's top hospitals. They feature dedicated cardiac wings with hybrid operating suites.

### Minimally Invasive Options (MICS CABG)
Traditional bypass requires splitting the breastbone and 7–10 days of hospital recovery. Minimally Invasive Cardiac Surgery (MICS CABG) allows surgeons to perform bypass through small incisions between the ribs — reducing hospital stay to 3–4 days and recovery time by half.

### Zero Waiting List
Your surgery can be scheduled within days of arrival. No waiting months for a slot.
