## Types of Spine Surgery Available in India

### 1. Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery (MISS)

Smaller incisions, less muscle damage, faster recovery. Indian hospitals have adopted MISS as the standard approach for most procedures.

**Benefits:**
- 1–2 inch incisions instead of 4–6 inches
- Hospital stay of 1–3 days instead of 5–7 days
- Return to work in 4–6 weeks instead of 12–16 weeks
- Less post-operative pain

### 2. Robotic-Assisted Spine Surgery

Hospitals in Kochi, Delhi, and Chennai offer robotic-assisted spine surgery using systems like Mazor X and Globus ExcelsiusGPS. These allow sub-millimetre precision in screw placement, reducing the risk of nerve damage and revision surgery.

### 3. Endoscopic Spine Surgery

For disc herniations and spinal stenosis, endoscopic techniques allow surgery through a tiny incision (less than 1 cm) using a camera — often as a day procedure with minimal recovery time.

### 4. Open Fusion & Deformity Correction

For complex cases — scoliosis, multiple-level degeneration, spinal tumours — India's top spine centres perform open reconstructive surgery with outcomes matching leading US and European hospitals.
