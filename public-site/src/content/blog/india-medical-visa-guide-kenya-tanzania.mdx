## First Things First: Are You Eligible?

Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria, Ghana, Rwanda, Ethiopia — all are on India's e-Medical Visa list. This means you can apply entirely online without visiting an embassy.

### e-Medical Visa at a Glance

| Detail | What You Need to Know |
|---|---|
| **Validity** | 60 days from first arrival in India |
| **Entries** | Triple entry (you can leave and re-enter) |
| **Processing time** | 24–72 hours (apply at least 4 days before travel) |
| **Cost** | Approximately $80 USD |
| **Companions** | Up to 2 family members can apply for e-Medical Attendant Visa |

## Step-by-Step: Getting Your Medical Visa

### Step 1: Get Your Hospital Invitation Letter

The hospital issues a formal letter confirming your name, passport number, proposed treatment, and admission date. **Heal India handles this for you.**

### Step 2: Gather Your Documents

| Document | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Passport | Valid for at least 6 months with 2 blank pages |
| Digital photo | White background, 2x2 inch, recent |
| Hospital invitation letter | Provided by Heal India |
| Medical reports | Referral letter from your doctor |
| Yellow fever certificate | Required if traveling from an endemic country |

### Step 3: Apply on the Official Portal

Visit **indianvisaonline.gov.in** and fill out the e-Medical Visa application form.

### Step 4: Book Your Flights

- From Nairobi: Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (6–8 hours total)
- From Dar es Salaam: Qatar Airways or Emirates (7–9 hours total)

### Step 5: Arrive in India

Present your e-Visa approval and hospital documents at immigration.
