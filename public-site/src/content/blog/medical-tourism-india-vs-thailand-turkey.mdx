## Deep Dive: When to Choose Each Destination

### Choose India If...

**You need complex, high-acuity surgery.** India's strength is in cardiac surgery, orthopedics, oncology, organ transplantation, and neurosurgery. With 41 JCI-accredited hospitals and surgeons who perform 400–500 cardiac procedures annually, India offers depth of expertise that Thailand and Turkey don't match in these categories.

**Cost savings matter most.** India is consistently 20–40% cheaper than Thailand and 15–30% cheaper than Turkey for the same procedure — and 70–90% cheaper than US prices.

**You want a holistic recovery.** Only India offers post-surgery Ayurvedic rehabilitation. The combination of advanced surgery at a JCI hospital followed by 2–3 weeks of Ayurvedic recovery in Kerala is a value proposition no other destination can match.

### Choose Thailand If...

**Cosmetic surgery or wellness is your priority.** Thailand has a strong reputation for cosmetic procedures and luxury recovery experiences. The recovery environment — beach resorts, spa retreats — is hard to beat.

**You prefer a tourist-friendly experience.** Thailand's tourism infrastructure is more developed than India's or Turkey's. If your treatment is straightforward and your priority is a vacation-like experience, Thailand excels.

**Distance from Asia-Pacific matters.** For patients from Australia, New Zealand, Japan, or Southeast Asia, Thailand is significantly closer than India or Turkey.

### Choose Turkey If...

**Hair transplant or cosmetic dentistry is your goal.** Turkey has the highest volume of hair transplant procedures globally and extremely competitive pricing for dental work.

**Proximity to Europe matters.** At 4–5 hours from London and 3 hours from Berlin, Turkey is the most accessible destination for European patients.

**You prefer a short stay.** Many cosmetic procedures in Turkey are designed for 3–5 day stays. India typically requires longer minimum stays (2–4 weeks for complex procedures).
