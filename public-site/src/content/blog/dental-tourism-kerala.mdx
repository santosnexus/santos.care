## Frequently Asked Questions

**What materials are used for implants and crowns in India?**
Our partner dental centres use the same FDA-approved materials as European clinics: Straumann and Nobel Biocare implants, zirconia and lithium disilicate crowns, and high-grade porcelain veneers.

**How do I know the quality is good?**
Kerala's top dental centres have international accreditation. Many dentists have trained or practised in the UK, US, or Australia. You can request virtual tours of the clinic and credentials of your dentist before travelling.

**What if I need follow-up care after returning home?**
Your dental centre will provide a complete treatment record, including implant serial numbers and post-care instructions. We can also coordinate with your local dentist for follow-up care.

**Is it safe to fly after dental implant surgery?**
For complex procedures, we recommend waiting 5–7 days before flying. Your dentist will clear you for travel. Light pressure changes during flight are normally fine after the first few days.

**Will I be in pain during the procedure?**
Dental implant surgery is performed under local anaesthesia. Most patients report less discomfort than a tooth extraction. Post-surgery pain is managed with medication and typically subsides within 48–72 hours.

**Can I combine dental treatment with Ayurveda?**
Yes. Kerala's Ayurveda centres offer therapies that complement dental recovery — herbal mouth rinses, anti-inflammatory massages, and stress-reducing treatments.
