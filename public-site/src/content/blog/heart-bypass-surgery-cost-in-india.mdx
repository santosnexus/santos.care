## A Week-by-Week Recovery Timeline

### Week 1 — Hospital (Days 1–7)

Day 1: Surgery day. You'll be in the cardiac ICU, waking up gradually. The team monitors your vitals continuously.

Day 2–3: You're moved from ICU to the ward. You'll be encouraged to sit up, then take a few steps with help. Pain is managed with medication.

Day 4–7: You're walking short distances, eating normally, and learning the breathing exercises you'll need at home.

### Week 2–3 — Recovery Stay in Kochi

Most international patients spend another 7–14 days in a serviced apartment near the hospital. Kochi's warm, humid climate is surprisingly good for recovery.

### Week 4 — Flying Home

Your surgeon clears you for air travel. You fly home with a detailed discharge summary, medication plan, and instructions for your local cardiologist.
