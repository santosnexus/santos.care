| Brachytherapy | Cervical, prostate, breast | All major centres |

### 2. Immunotherapy & Targeted Therapy

Indian oncologists have extensive experience with the latest immunotherapy agents (pembrolizumab, nivolumab, atezolizumab) and targeted therapies — at significantly lower cost than in Western countries. A typical immunotherapy cycle costs $1,500–$3,000 in India compared to $10,000–$20,000 in the US.

### 3. Robotic Surgery (Da Vinci)

For prostate, gynaecological, thoracic, and head & neck cancers, robotic-assisted surgery offers:
- Smaller incisions
- Less blood loss
- Shorter hospital stay (2–4 days)
- Faster return to normal activities
