## How to Choose a Cosmetic Surgeon — The Right Way

1. **Board certification** — Indian Association of Plastic Surgeons (IAPS) or equivalent
2. **Portfolio review** — Before-and-after photos of similar cases
3. **Hospital accreditation** — NABH or JCI
4. **Clear communication** — Surgeon explains risks realistically
5. **Itemized quote** — Every charge broken down
6. **Patient reviews** — Real testimonials from international patients
