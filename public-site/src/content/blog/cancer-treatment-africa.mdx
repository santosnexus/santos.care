## What Advanced Cancer Treatments Are Available in India?

### Robotic Surgical Oncology
Indian cancer centres use the Da Vinci Xi robotic system for extreme precision in complex surgeries — prostate, lung, liver, pancreatic, and gynecologic cancers. Smaller incisions, less blood loss, faster recovery.

### Precision Radiation
- **CyberKnife** — Robotic radiosurgery for tumours in hard-to-reach areas
- **TrueBeam** — Image-guided radiation that adjusts to tumour movement during breathing
- **Proton Therapy** — Targeted radiation that spares healthy tissue (available at select centres)

### Immunotherapy & Targeted Therapy
India offers the latest FDA-approved monoclonal antibodies and immunotherapies — including PD-1 inhibitors like Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) — at a fraction of Western prices.
