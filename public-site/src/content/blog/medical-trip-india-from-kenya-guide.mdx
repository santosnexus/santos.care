## Step-by-Step: Planning Your Medical Trip

### Step 1: Initial Consultation

Send your medical reports via WhatsApp or email. A specialist from our partner network reviews them within 24 hours and provides a written treatment plan with transparent pricing.

**What to send:**
- Diagnostic reports (X-rays, MRI, CT scans, lab results)
- Doctor's referral letter
- Brief medical history (current medications, allergies, chronic conditions)

### Step 2: Treatment Plan & Hospital Selection

You receive:
- Recommended treatment options
- 2–3 hospital choices with itemized cost breakdowns
- Surgeon profiles and credentials
- Expected recovery timeline

Take your time comparing. We don't rush decisions.

### Step 3: Medical Visa Application

Kenya is on India's e-Medical Visa list. You apply entirely online.

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| Processing time | 24–72 hours (apply 7 days before travel) |
| Cost | Approximately $80 USD |
| Validity | 60 days from arrival, triple entry |
| Documents needed | Passport (6+ months validity), digital photo, hospital invitation letter |

**Heal India provides your hospital invitation letter** — the most critical document — within 24–48 hours of treatment plan confirmation.

### Step 4: Book Flights

| Route | Airlines | Duration | Cost (Return) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nairobi (NBO) → Kochi (COK) | Kenya Airways, Emirates, Ethiopian | 7.5 hrs direct or 9–12 hrs via Dubai/Addis | KES 80,000–150,000 |
| Nairobi (NBO) → Delhi (DEL) | Kenya Airways, Air India | 7.5 hrs direct | KES 85,000–160,000 |

**Tip:** Book 4–6 weeks in advance for the best fares. Mid-week flights (Tuesday–Thursday) are typically cheaper.

### Step 5: Arrive in India

Your coordinator meets you at the airport arrivals hall with a name board. Private transport takes you directly to the hospital or pre-arranged accommodation.

### Step 6: Treatment & Recovery

| Phase | Duration | Support |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-op workup | 1–2 days | Tests, surgeon consultation, anaesthetist review |
| Procedure | 1 day (or scheduled surgery) | Dedicated coordinator bedside |
| Hospital stay | 3–7 days | Private room, meals, nursing, physiotherapy |
| Recovery in India | 1–3 weeks | Serviced apartment, follow-up visits, WhatsApp support |
| Return home | — | Discharge summary, medication plan, remote follow-up |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does the medical visa take for Kenyans?**
A: Most applications are approved within 24–72 hours. We recommend applying at least 7 days before travel.

**Q: Can my spouse come with me?**
A: Yes. Spouse and children can apply for an e-Medical Attendant Visa.

**Q: Will there be a language barrier?**
A: All our partner hospitals have English-speaking medical staff. Swahili-speaking interpreters are available on request.

**Q: What if I have complications after returning to Kenya?**
A: We coordinate remote follow-up with your surgeon and local Kenyan doctor. Most hospitals offer WhatsApp-based post-operative support.

**Q: How do I pay for treatment?**
A: Bank transfer, credit card, or SWIFT payment. Most hospitals accept international payments.
