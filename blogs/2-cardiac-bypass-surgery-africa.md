## Minimal Access Bypass Surgery (MICS CABG)
Traditionally, bypass surgery requires splitting the breastbone (sternotomy). However, Indian hospitals specialize in **Minimally Invasive Coronary Artery Surgery (MICS CABG)**. 
* **How it works:** The surgeon accesses the heart through a small incision between the ribs.
* **Benefits:** Less pain, minimal blood loss, lower risk of infection, and a significantly faster recovery (discharge in 3-4 days instead of 7-10 days). This is highly beneficial for international travelers who want to return home quickly.
